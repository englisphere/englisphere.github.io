Business and Technology Vocabulary (Advanced Level)

1. Key Vocabulary

Word / Phrase Meaning Example Sentence
EntrepreneurSomeone who starts and runs a new business.Many entrepreneurs use technology to launch startups.
ScalabilityThe ability of a business to grow without being hindered by its structure.The app’s scalability impressed investors.
MonetizationTurning something into money or income.The company seeks ways for the platform's monetization.
Disruptive InnovationAn innovation that significantly alters an industry.Streaming services were a disruptive innovation to cable TV.
CrowdfundingRaising small amounts of money from many people via the internet.The startup used crowdfunding to finance their first product.
AutomationThe use of technology to perform tasks without human help.Automation has transformed manufacturing industries.
Artificial Intelligence (AI)Computer systems that mimic human intelligence.AI is revolutionizing healthcare and business operations.
BlockchainA digital ledger technology mainly known for cryptocurrencies.Blockchain ensures secure and transparent transactions.
E-commerceBuying and selling goods/services online.E-commerce saw a boom during the pandemic.
Venture CapitalFunding given to startups with strong growth potential.The company secured venture capital to expand internationally.
