ECC Grammar Corner: Simple Present Tense

What is the Simple Present Tense?

Simple Present Tense is used to describe:

  • Daily routines
  • General truths
  • Fixed schedules
  • Facts

Formula

1. (+) Positive Sentences

Subject + Verb 1 (s/es) + Object

SubjectExample
I/You/We/TheyI eat breakfast at 6 AM.
He/She/ItShe eats breakfast at 6 AM.

Add s/es only for He/She/It!

2. (-) Negative Sentences
