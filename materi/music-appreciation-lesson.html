📘 Music Appreciation Lesson: “I’ll Die with a Smile” – Bruno Mars

🎯 Learning Objectives:

By the end of the lesson, students will be able to:

  • Understand the meaning and message of the song
  • Express personal reactions and emotions using appropriate vocabulary
  • Analyze lyrics and connect them to life experiences
  • Practice listening comprehension and critical thinking

🧠 Pre-Listening Discussion:

Ask students:

  • What kind of music do you listen to when you're feeling emotional or thoughtful?
  • Do you think songs can teach us life lessons? Why or why not?
  • What do you think a song titled “I’ll Die with a Smile” might be about?

💡 Key Vocabulary (from the song):

Word/Phrase Meaning
SmileA facial expression of happiness
RegretA feeling of sadness about something done
GoodbyeA way to end a conversation or connection
CherishTo hold something dear or valuable
GratefulFeeling thankful
Final breathThe last moment of life
