HOW TO CREATE AN INTERESTING SELF-INTORDUCTION?

Diposting oleh Ayu Ukhti Muslimah pada 20 April 2025

Materi 3 - How to Create an Interesting Self-Introduction?

Hi guys! Have you ever introduced yourself in English before? And how do you feel when you have to speak in front of people—nervous, excited, or maybe both?

In this session, we’ll explore what makes a self-introduction effective and engaging. Let’s find out how to introduce yourself with confidence and make a great first impression.

To make your self-introduction more interesting, you can combine two important parts: the Basic Structure of Self-Introduction and the Extended Self-Introduction. You can check out simple examples of both in the tables below!

Use The Variety Phrases and Expressions

When introducing yourself, you don't always have to use the same sentence structure. Using a variety of expressions can make your self-introduction sound more natural, friendly, and engaging. Here's a list of useful alternative phrases for different parts of a self-introduction:

1. Saying Your Name

  • My name is Gita.
  • I'm Agita Nindi Sofiyani.
  • You can call me Gita.
  • Let me introduce myself. I'm Gita.

2. Saying Where You're From

  • I'm from Jember, East Java.
  • I come from a small town called Jember.
  • I was born and raised in Jember.
  • Currently, I live in Jember for my studies.

3. Talking About Date of Birth / Age

  • I was born on January 3rd, 2007.
  • I’m 17 years old.
  • My birthday is on the 3rd of January.
  • I turned 17 this year.

4. Talking About Job / School

  • I’m a high school student.
  • I’m currently studying at SMA Negeri Kalisat.
  • I’m in my final year of senior high school.
  • I’m a student majoring in language and culture.

5. Talking About Hobbies

  • I enjoy watching K-dramas.
  • I love listening to music and relaxing.
  • In my free time, I like to read or scroll on social media.
  • One of my hobbies is traveling.

Example of Self-Introduction

Text 1

Hello everyone!

Let me introduce myself. My name is Gita, and you can just call me Gita. I was born on January 3rd, 2007, which means I’m currently 18 years old.

I come from Jember, a nice town in East Java. I’m currently studying at SMAN Kalisat, and I’m in my final year of high school.

In my free time, I enjoy watching movies and listening to music. I also like spending time with my friends, either chatting or hanging out at cafés. One fun fact about me is that I always prefer ordering food online instead of cooking!

In the future, I dream of becoming a successful person who can travel the world. Nice to meet you all, and thank you for listening!

Text 2

Hi guys!

I’m Gita, but feel free to just call me Gita. I was born in Jember on January 3rd, 2007. Yep, I’m 18 years old now!

Right now, I go to SMAN Kalisat, and I’m a senior there. Honestly, high school life is busy, but also pretty fun—especially when I get to hang out with friends or watch my favorite shows.

When I’m not busy with schoolwork, you’ll probably find me listening to music or just chilling at home. Oh, and here’s something funny: I almost never cook because I love ordering food online. It’s just so easy and delicious!

My biggest dream? I’d love to travel the world someday and experience different cultures. Thanks for getting to know me!
