Simple Future Tense

Apa itu Simple Future Tense? Simple Future Tense digunakan untuk menyatakan sesuatu yang akan terjadi di masa depan.

1. Contoh situasi:

  • Rencana masa depan
  • Prediksi
  • Keputusan spontan

2. Rumus Simple Future Tense

Bentuk Kalimat Rumus Contoh
(+) Positive Subject + will + Verb 1 I will go to school.
(-) Negative Subject + will not (won’t) + Verb 1 She won’t come tomorrow.
(?) Question Will + Subject + Verb 1 + ? Will they join the trip?

3. Kapan Menggunakan Simple Future Tense?

  • Keputusan spontan (baru diputuskan saat bicara)
    Ex: I’m thirsty. I’ll get some water.
  • Prediksi tanpa bukti pasti
    Ex: It will rain tomorrow.
  • Janji atau tawaran
    Ex: I will help you with your homework.
  • Rencana umum di masa depan
    Ex: They will travel next year.

4. Kata Keterangan Waktu yang Sering Digunakan

  • Tomorrow
  • Next week / next month / next year
  • In a few minutes / in two days
  • Soon
  • Later

Contoh: We will visit grandma next Sunday.

5. Contoh Kalimat

Positive:

  • She will visit her cousin next week.
  • I will study harder.

Negative:

  • He won’t eat spicy food.
  • We will not leave early.

Interrogative:

  • Will you come to the party?
  • Will she call me?
