News Reading Activity: Be the News Anchor!

What is News Reading?

News reading is the practice of reading news articles aloud with the correct pronunciation, intonation, and expression, just like a news presenter or anchor.

It helps you:

  • Improve reading fluency
  • Build confidence in public speaking
  • Learn new vocabulary and formal structures
