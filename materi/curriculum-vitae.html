What is a CV?

A CV (Curriculum Vitae) is a document that summarizes your educational background, work experience, skills, and other qualifications. It is usually used when applying for a job, scholarship, or internship.

Parts of a CV (Common Sections)

Section Description
Personal Information Full name, date of birth, contact details (email, phone number), address
Objective A brief statement about your career goals or purpose of applying
Education List of schools/universities attended, including dates and majors
Work Experience Any job or internship experience, with responsibilities and time period
Skills Abilities such as languages, computer skills, public speaking, etc.
Achievements / Awards Any competitions, certificates, or recognitions
Hobbies & Interests Optional; shows your personality and interests
References People who can recommend or verify your skills (optional)

Tips and Tricks for Making Impressive CV

  • Start with a clear objective
    Tulis tujuan kamu dengan ringkas dan kuat. Hindari terlalu umum. Misalnya:
    ‚ÄúA highly motivated student seeking to develop communication and leadership skills through internship or school projects.‚Äù
  • Use Simple & Professional Language
    Gunakan bahasa Inggris yang jelas, sopan, dan tidak bertele-tele. Hindari singkatan atau bahasa gaul.
  • Highlight Your Strengths and Skills
    Jangan takut menonjolkan kelebihanmu, meskipun kamu belum pernah bekerja. Fokus pada soft skills dan hard skills:
    • Public speaking
    • Time management
    • Writing
    • Digital skills (PowerPoint, Canva, etc)
  • Include School Activities
    Kegiatan sekolah, organisasi, volunteer, dan lomba juga bisa masuk! Itu menunjukkan kamu aktif dan punya inisiatif.
  • Use Action Words (Power Verbs)
    Gunakan kata kerja aktif seperti:
    • Organized
    • Participated
    • Created
    • Contributed
    • Achieved
    Contoh:
    ‚ÄúOrganized a class project on environmental awareness.‚Äù
    ‚ÄúParticipated in the school‚Äôs English Debate Club.‚Äù
  • Make It Neat and Well-Formatted
    Gunakan format yang rapi, konsisten, dan mudah dibaca:
    • Gunakan heading yang jelas
    • Jangan terlalu banyak warna
    • Gunakan font profesional seperti Arial atau Calibri
  • Keep It Short
    Hanya tampilkan informasi yang relevan dan penting.
  • Double Check Your Grammar
    Kesalahan grammar atau ejaan bisa bikin CV-mu terlihat tidak profesional. Cek ulang sebelum kirim atau cetak.
  • Update It Regularly
    Setiap kali kamu ikut kegiatan atau lomba baru, langsung tambahkan ke CV!
